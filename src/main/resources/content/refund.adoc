== Our Commitment

At Village Compute, we take pride in the quality of our custom calendars. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, we're here to help.

== Custom Products Policy

Because our calendars are custom-made with your personal photos and content, we handle returns and refunds on a case-by-case basis. However, we guarantee:

* **Quality Assurance**: Every calendar must meet our quality standards
* **Accurate Printing**: Your photos and content will be printed as previewed
* **Safe Delivery**: Products should arrive undamaged

== Eligible for Refund

You may be eligible for a full refund or replacement if:

=== Print Quality Issues

* Significant color discrepancies from the preview
* Blurry or distorted images (not due to low-resolution source photos)
* Misprints, smudges, or ink problems
* Missing pages or incorrect binding

=== Production Errors

* Wrong calendar format delivered
* Incorrect dates or layout errors caused by our system
* Missing customizations that were part of your order

=== Shipping Damage

* Calendars that arrive bent, torn, or water-damaged
* Crushed or damaged packaging affecting the product

== Not Eligible for Refund

Refunds typically cannot be provided for:

* Low quality due to low-resolution source images (we provide warnings during upload)
* Typos or errors in text you provided
* Change of mind after order has been printed
* Color variations within normal printing tolerances
* Orders placed with incorrect shipping information

== How to Request a Refund

=== Step 1: Contact Us

Email us at **refunds@villagecompute.com** within **14 days** of receiving your order. Include:

* Your order number
* Description of the issue
* Photos showing the problem (for quality/damage issues)

=== Step 2: Review Process

Our team will review your request within **2-3 business days** and respond with:

* Approval for refund or replacement
* Request for additional information
* Explanation if the request doesn't qualify

=== Step 3: Resolution

If approved, you can choose:

* **Full Refund**: Returned to original payment method within 5-7 business days
* **Replacement**: We'll reprint and ship a new calendar at no cost
* **Store Credit**: Receive credit for a future order (with 10% bonus)

== Cancellation Policy

=== Before Production

Orders can be cancelled with a full refund if production hasn't started (typically within 24 hours of placing the order).

=== During Production

Once production begins, orders cannot be cancelled. However, you may be eligible for the refund process described above if there are quality issues.

== Exchange Policy

We don't offer direct exchanges, but if you receive a defective product, we'll happily send a replacement as described in our refund process.
