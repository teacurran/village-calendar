== Shipping Overview

Village Compute Calendar ships to addresses within the United States. We partner with reliable carriers to ensure your custom calendars arrive safely and on time.

== Processing Time

=== Standard Processing

Orders typically require **5-7 business days** for production before shipping. This includes:

* Quality printing of your custom calendar
* Careful inspection and quality control
* Professional packaging

=== Rush Processing

Need it faster? Rush processing is available for an additional fee:

* **Express (3-5 business days)**: +$10
* **Priority (1-2 business days)**: +$20

_Rush processing times do not include shipping transit time._

== Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by:

1. Logging into your account
2. Visiting the "Orders" section
3. Clicking on your order to view tracking details

== Delivery Information

=== Delivery Address

Please ensure your shipping address is accurate and complete. We are not responsible for orders delivered to incorrect addresses provided by the customer.

=== Delivery Attempts

Carriers typically make multiple delivery attempts. If delivery is unsuccessful, your package may be:

* Left at a nearby pickup location
* Returned to sender after 5 business days

=== Signature Requirements

Orders over $100 may require a signature upon delivery for security purposes.

== Holiday Shipping

During peak holiday seasons (November-December), we recommend:

* Ordering early to ensure timely delivery
* Selecting expedited shipping for guaranteed delivery dates
* Checking our holiday shipping deadlines (published annually)

== International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within the United States. We're working on expanding our shipping options and will announce international shipping when available.

== Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit:

1. Contact us within **48 hours** of delivery (or expected delivery)
2. Provide photos of any damage
3. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue
4. Replacement or refund will be processed accordingly
