== Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by:

1. Logging into your account
2. Visiting the "Orders" section
3. Clicking on your order to view tracking details

== Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit:

1. Contact us within **48 hours** of delivery (or expected delivery)
2. Provide photos of any damage
3. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue
4. Replacement or refund will be processed accordingly
