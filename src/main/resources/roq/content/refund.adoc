== Our Commitment

At Village Calendar, we take pride in the quality of our custom-printed calendars. Your satisfaction is important to us, and we stand behind our products.

== Custom Products

=== Understanding Custom Orders

All Village Calendar products are **custom printed to order**. Each calendar is uniquely created based on your:

* Selected year and date range
* Holiday and event selections
* Moon phase preferences
* Color and style choices
* Custom events and dates you've added

Because of the personalized nature of our products, we have specific policies regarding returns and refunds.

== Refund Eligibility

=== Eligible for Full Refund

You may receive a full refund if:

* **Order not shipped**: Cancel before your order enters production (typically within 24 hours of placing the order)
* **Printing defects**: Calendar arrives with printing errors, smudges, or quality issues
* **Shipping damage**: Product is damaged during shipping
* **Wrong product**: You receive a calendar different from what you ordered

=== Eligible for Partial Refund or Reprint

* **Minor defects**: Small imperfections that don't significantly impact the product
* **Color variations**: Significant color differences from preview (Note: minor color variation between screen and print is normal)

=== Not Eligible for Refund

* **Change of mind**: You no longer want the calendar after it's printed
* **Incorrect information entered**: Errors in custom events or dates you provided
* **Recipient refusal**: Gift recipient refuses delivery
* **Normal wear**: Damage occurring after delivery

== How to Request a Refund

=== Step 1: Contact Us

Email us at returns@villagecompute.com within **14 days** of receiving your order. Include:

* Your order number
* Description of the issue
* Photos of any defects or damage

=== Step 2: Review Process

* We'll review your request within 2 business days
* We may request additional information or photos
* We'll provide instructions if a return is required

=== Step 3: Resolution

If approved, we will offer one of the following:

* **Full refund** to your original payment method
* **Replacement calendar** printed and shipped at no cost
* **Partial refund** for minor issues

== Return Shipping

=== Defective or Damaged Products

* We provide a prepaid return shipping label
* No cost to you for returning defective items

=== Other Returns

* If you're returning for reasons other than defects, return shipping is your responsibility
* We recommend using tracked shipping

== Refund Processing

=== Timeline

* Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of approval
* Credit card refunds may take an additional 3-5 business days to appear on your statement
* Bank processing times vary

=== Refund Method

* Refunds are issued to the original payment method
* We cannot refund to a different payment method

== Order Cancellation

=== Before Production

* Orders can be cancelled for a full refund before entering production
* Contact us immediately at orders@villagecompute.com
* Cancellation requests are processed in the order received

=== During Production

* Once production begins, cancellation may not be possible
* We'll do our best to accommodate requests but cannot guarantee

=== After Shipping

* Orders that have shipped cannot be cancelled
* See our return policy for post-delivery options

== Quality Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our calendars:

* Professional-grade printing
* Premium paper stock
* Careful packaging and shipping
* Quality control inspection before shipping

If you're not satisfied with the quality, we'll make it right.

== Contact Us

For refund or return questions:

* **Email**: returns@villagecompute.com
* **Subject Line**: Include your order number
* **Response Time**: Within 2 business days

We're here to help ensure you're happy with your Village Calendar purchase.
