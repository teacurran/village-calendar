== Shipping Overview

Village Calendar ships custom-printed wall calendars throughout the United States. Each calendar is printed to order to ensure the highest quality and freshness.

== Processing Time

=== Standard Orders

* **Processing**: 5-7 business days
* Orders are printed and prepared for shipment within this timeframe
* You will receive an email notification when your order ships

=== Rush Orders

* **Rush Processing**: 2-3 business days (when available)
* Rush processing may be available for an additional fee
* Select rush processing at checkout if available

NOTE: Processing times do not include shipping transit time.

== Shipping Carriers

We ship via:

* **USPS** (United States Postal Service)
* **UPS** (United Parcel Service)
* **FedEx**

The carrier is selected based on package size, destination, and shipping speed selected.

== Order Tracking

=== Tracking Information

* Tracking numbers are emailed when your order ships
* Track your order through our website or directly with the carrier
* Allow 24-48 hours for tracking information to update after shipment

=== Check Order Status

To check your order status:

1. Log in to your Village Calendar account
2. Navigate to "My Orders"
3. Click on your order to view status and tracking

Or use the order status link in your confirmation email.

== Packaging

Your calendar is carefully packaged to ensure safe delivery:

* Calendars are rolled and placed in sturdy mailing tubes
* Protective end caps prevent damage during transit
* "Handle With Care" labels are applied to packaging

== Delivery Details

=== Delivery Address

* Please ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate
* We ship to residential and business addresses
* PO Boxes may have limited shipping options

=== Signature Requirements

* Standard orders do not require signature
* High-value orders may require signature confirmation
* You may request signature confirmation at checkout

=== Delivery Issues

If you experience delivery issues:

* Contact the carrier with your tracking number
* Contact us at shipping@villagecompute.com
* We will work with you to resolve any delivery problems

== International Shipping

We currently ship only within the United States.

International shipping may be available in the future. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

== Shipping Restrictions

We cannot ship to:

* APO/FPO addresses (limited carrier support)
* US Territories may have extended delivery times

== Holiday Shipping

=== Peak Season Notice

During peak holiday seasons (November-December):

* Processing times may be extended by 2-3 additional days
* We recommend ordering early to ensure delivery before holidays
* Expedited shipping is recommended for time-sensitive orders

=== Holiday Deadlines

Check our website for specific holiday ordering deadlines to ensure delivery before major holidays.

== Contact Us

For shipping questions or concerns:

* **Email**: shipping@villagecompute.com
* **Order Issues**: Include your order number in all correspondence

We typically respond within 1-2 business days.
